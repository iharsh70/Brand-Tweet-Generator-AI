## Brand Voice Analysis

**Tone**: Unapologetically bold and motivational with a competitive edge that transforms everyday movement into athletic greatness

**Target Audience**: Ambitious athletes and fitness-driven Gen Z/Millennials (16-35) who see sport as identity, not just activity

**Content Themes**:
- Athletic excellence & competition
- Everyday athletes & inclusivity
- Sustainability (Move to Zero)
- Digital fitness community

**Voice Guidelines**:
- Use short, punchy sentences. Declarative over descriptive. Every word must earn its place.
- Speak to the athlete in everyone -- from Olympic champions to morning joggers. Elevate the ordinary.
- Challenge the audience. Provoke. Make them feel like sitting still is the only wrong move.
- Reference cultural moments and athlete stories, never product specs. Sell the feeling, not the shoe.
